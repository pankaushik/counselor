Individual Counseling (1:1 Sessions)

Mind Us offers one-to-one counselling sessions that provide a safe, confidential, and non judgmental space for individuals to discuss their thoughts, emotions, and personal challenges. These sessions focus on concerns such as anxiety, stress, life transitions, and adjustment difficulties, while helping individuals develop coping strategies and build emotional resilience. Sessions are tailored to each person’s unique experiences and needs. The duration of these sessions would be 60 minutes.

Group Sessions / Support Groups

Mind Us also offers small group counselling and support group sessions (up to 5 participants). These sessions provide a safe and supportive space where individuals can share experiences, learn from one another, and feel less alone in their challenges. Group settings can help participants build coping strategies, gain new perspectives, and develop a sense of community and support.
Group sessions are held on fixed days — Tuesdays and Fridays.

Wellbeing Services for Educational Institutions

Mind Us offers wellbeing services for schools and educational institutions, including counselling support for students and staff. These services aim to support emotional wellbeing, address academic and personal challenges, and promote a healthier school environment. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Mind Us offers Employee Assistance Programs (EAP) to support the mental health and wellbeing of employees within organizations. This includes individual counselling sessions for company staff, providing a confidential space to discuss personal or work-related concerns.

We also organise workshops and webinars on selected wellbeing topics, such as managing burnout, stress management, and maintaining work–life balance. These sessions are designed to be interactive and engaging, incorporating reflective and introspective activities that encourage practical learning and self-awareness, rather than focusing only on theoretical content.
